Our Story

Crafted With Passion

For over 130 years, Goats of Kaffa has preserved the ancient coffee traditions of Ethiopia's Kaffa region the birthplace of coffee. Our story began when our founder Alemayehu Kassa iscovered wild coffee berries being eaten by goats in the highlands, noticing their energetic behavior.

Today, we continue this legacy by working directly with 500+ smallholder farmers across Ethiopia
